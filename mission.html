OUR MISSION

PRISM: Proximity & Rendezvous Integrated Simulation Model

Our mission is to advance space technology through an innovative low-cost satellite demonstration model, pushing the boundaries of what's possible for Rendezvous Proximity Operations (RPO). We are dedicated to creating cutting-edge solutions that contribute to scientific discovery, space exploration, and technological advancement.

Through collaborative research, rigorous engineering, and passionate dedication to excellence, we aim to enable and inspire the next generation of space professionals while delivering meaningful contributions to the aerospace community.
